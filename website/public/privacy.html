Privacy Policy

Effective: April 7, 2026

Detriment is a WiFi security scanner that runs on your device. We built it with privacy as a core principle.

What stays on your device

All of the following data is stored locally and never leaves your device:

  • IP addresses of devices on your network
  • MAC addresses and device identifiers
  • Device hostnames and custom names you assign
  • Your WiFi network name (SSID)
  • Scan history and Detriment Scores
  • Trusted device preferences

What we collect

When you run a scan, Detriment sends anonymized device type information to our server to improve detection accuracy. This includes:

  • The first 3 bytes of MAC addresses (manufacturer prefix only — this identifies "Apple" or "Samsung", not your specific device)
  • Open port numbers

We do not collect your IP address, full MAC address, device names, network name, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

Location data

Detriment requests location permission solely because Apple requires it to read your WiFi network name and security type. We do not track, store, or transmit your location.

Third-party services

Detriment does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Data retention

Anonymized device type data sent to our server is retained for up to 12 months to improve our detection database, then deleted.

Children

Detriment does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
