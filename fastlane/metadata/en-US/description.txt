Know exactly who's on your WiFi — and what they're up to.

Detriment scans your home network in seconds and shows you every connected device: phones, laptops, cameras, smart home gadgets, and anything suspicious. Each device gets a plain-English risk assessment so you don't need to be a tech expert to stay safe.

YOUR DETRIMENT SCORE
Get a clear A-F grade showing how secure your network is. The score breaks down into four categories: WiFi security, unknown devices, open ports, and risky devices. Tap any device to see exactly what's wrong and how to fix it.

WHAT IT FINDS
- Every device on your WiFi, identified by name and manufacturer
- Open ports that could let attackers in
- Cameras streaming video that others might watch
- Devices with insecure remote access enabled
- Cheap IoT chips with known security problems
- Mystery devices that can't be identified

STAY INFORMED
- Get notified instantly when a new device joins your network
- Background monitoring checks for changes even when the app is closed
- Home screen widget shows your score and new device alerts at a glance
- Scan history tracks your network security over time

TRUST & MANAGE
- Mark your own devices as trusted so they stop flagging
- Give devices custom names for easy identification
- Share your scan report with family or your IT person

PRIVACY FIRST
- No account required
- Your network data stays on your device
- Only anonymized device types are shared to improve detection (never IPs, names, or MAC addresses)
- No ads, no tracking, no subscriptions
